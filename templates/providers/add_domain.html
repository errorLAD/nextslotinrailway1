Common Questions
What's the difference between CNAME and A records?
CNAME: Points your domain to another domain (e.g., app.nextslot.in). Easier to set up and maintain.
A Record: Points your domain directly to an IP address. More stable if IP doesn't change.
Recommendation: Use CNAME for simplicity.
How long does it take for my domain to work?
DNS propagation typically takes 5-30 minutes, but can take up to 48 hours. SSL certificate generation takes 5-15 minutes after DNS verification. You can check DNS status at mxtoolbox.com.
Do I need to renew my SSL certificate?
No! Your SSL certificate is provided free via Let's Encrypt and automatically renews every 90 days. You don't need to do anything.
Can I have multiple domains?
Yes! As a PRO user, you can add multiple domains and choose which one is your primary domain. All your existing bookings will work on all domains.
