DigitalOcean Multi-Domain DNS Setup

How it works: Each provider's custom domain needs a CNAME record pointing to their subdomain on nextslot.in. This allows you to use your own domain while maintaining our DigitalOcean hosting infrastructure.

Example for okmentor.in:
okmentor.in CNAME ‚Üí okmentor.nextslot.in CNAME ‚Üí app.ondigitalocean.app

Time to propagate: Usually 5-30 minutes, but can take up to 48 hours.

CNAME Record (Recommended - Add at Your Registrar)
Your Provider Subdomain: {{ provider.unique_booking_url }}.nextslot.in

Type CNAME

Leave as root (@) or use www, depending on registrar

Step-by-Step Setup Guide
üìù Follow these steps in order:
  1. 1. Log in to Your Domain Registrar

    Access your account at your domain provider (GoDaddy, Namecheap, AWS Route53, Google Domains, Bluehost, HostGator, etc.)

  2. 2. Navigate to DNS Settings

    Look for "DNS Management", "DNS Settings", "DNS Records", or "Manage DNS" (the name varies by registrar)

  3. 3. Add CNAME Record (Primary Method)
    Type: CNAME
    Name/Host: @ or www
    Value/Target: {{ cname_target }}
    TTL: 3600 (or "Auto")

    Copy and paste the values exactly as shown. The "@" symbol means root domain.

  4. 4. Add TXT Verification Record (Recommended)
    Type: TXT
    Name: _booking-verify
    Value: {{ verification_code }}
    TTL: 3600

    This verifies you own the domain. Copy the entire verification code exactly.

  5. 5. Save All DNS Records

    Click "Save", "Update", or "Apply" to save your DNS changes

  6. 6. Wait for DNS Propagation

    DNS changes can take 5 minutes to 48 hours to propagate. Usually it's 30 minutes. You can check status at mxtoolbox.com

  7. 7. Verify Your Domain

    Click the "Verify Domain" button below to check if DNS is configured correctly
