Setup Instructions
  1. Go to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, Route53, etc)
  2. Find DNS Settings - Look for DNS Management or DNS Settings
  3. Add CNAME Record:
    Type: CNAME
    Name: @ (or www)
    Value: {{ cname_target }}
    TTL: 3600 (or Auto)
  4. Save - Click Save/Apply
  5. Wait for DNS - Takes 5 minutes to 24 hours (usually 30 minutes)
